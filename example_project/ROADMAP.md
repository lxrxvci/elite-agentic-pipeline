# Roadmap

*Outcome-based roadmap derived from the product brief: a SaaS dashboard that helps freelancers track time, invoice clients, and get paid faster.*

---

## Now

> Focus: validate the core loop and remove the biggest friction in getting from time logged to money received.

1. **Freelancers can create and send an invoice in under 60 seconds.**
   - *Brief link:* Invoicing is a stated core capability; speed reduces admin burden.
   - *Assumption:* Time spent creating invoices is a major pain point for freelancers using spreadsheets.

2. **Freelancers log at least 80% of their billable hours without leaving the dashboard.**
   - *Brief link:* Time tracking is the upstream activity that feeds invoicing and payment.
   - *Assumption:* If logging is too hard, freelancers under-bill and churn.

3. **Freelancers receive their first payment through the dashboard within 14 days of signup.**
   - *Brief link:* "Get paid faster" is the primary outcome promised.
   - *Assumption:* Early payment success is the strongest activation signal for long-term retention.

---

## Next

> Focus: expand value within the freelancer’s existing workflow and increase the frequency of paid invoices.

4. **Freelancers reduce average time-to-payment by 30% compared to their previous tool.**
   - *Brief link:* Faster payment is a core value proposition.
   - *Assumption:* Automated reminders and clear payment terms meaningfully shorten collection cycles.

5. **Freelancers recover at least one previously missed billable hour per week.**
   - *Brief link:* Time tracking supports accurate billing.
   - *Assumption:* Integrations with calendar and project tools capture hours that manual entry misses.

6. **Freelancers manage multiple clients without losing visibility into who owes what.**
   - *Brief link:* Dashboard implies a unified view of time, invoices, and clients.
   - *Assumption:* Cash-flow anxiety is driven more by visibility than by total volume.

---

## Later

> Focus: scale and differentiate once the core loop is proven.

7. **Freelancers expand revenue per client through upsell insights (e.g., retainer usage, project margins).**
   - *Brief link:* Dashboard can evolve from admin tool to revenue intelligence.
   - *Assumption:* Mature freelancers will pay for insights that help them price and package services.

8. **Freelancers get paid for work done outside the product via expense and reimbursement tracking.**
   - *Brief link:* Invoicing naturally extends to reimbursable expenses.
   - *Assumption:* Expense pass-through is a frequent adjacency for service freelancers.

9. **Freelancers win new business through embedded proposals, contracts, and e-signatures.**
   - *Brief link:* Pre-sale workflow is adjacent to time tracking and invoicing.
   - *Assumption:* A unified pre- and post-sale workflow reduces tool switching for growing independents.
