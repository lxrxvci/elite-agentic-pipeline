# Workflow: Continuous Discovery Loop

## Purpose

Replace big-bang research with a weekly discipline of customer conversation and assumption validation.

## Trigger

- New product brief
- Weekly cadence
- New opportunity spotted in usage data or support tickets

## Participants

- Product Strategist (lead)
- UX Researcher
- Tech Lead (feasibility sniff test)

## Steps

1. **Identify assumptions**
   - Product Strategist and UX Researcher list riskiest assumptions in `DISCOVERY/ASSUMPTIONS.md`.
   - Rank by impact and uncertainty.

2. **Conduct customer conversations**
   - UX Researcher runs 3–5 conversations per week.
   - Capture notes in `DISCOVERY/INTERVIEW_NOTES/`.

3. **Synthesize insights**
   - UX Researcher updates `DISCOVERY/INTERVIEW_SYNTHESIS.md`.
   - Extract jobs, pains, and gains.

4. **Update Opportunity Solution Tree**
   - Product Strategist adds validated opportunities to `DISCOVERY/OST.md`.
   - Link evidence to each opportunity.

5. **Feasibility sniff test**
   - Tech Lead provides 5-minute go/no-go/spike recommendation per opportunity.

6. **Prioritize opportunities**
   - Product Strategist ranks opportunities against North Star and business outcomes.
   - Move top opportunities to the backlog as shaped bets.

## Exit criteria

- `DISCOVERY/OST.md` updated with evidence links
- `DISCOVERY/ASSUMPTIONS.md` and `DISCOVERY/INTERVIEW_NOTES/` maintained by UX Researcher
- Top 1–3 opportunities ready for shaping
- No opportunity added without evidence

## Frequency

Weekly, continuously.
