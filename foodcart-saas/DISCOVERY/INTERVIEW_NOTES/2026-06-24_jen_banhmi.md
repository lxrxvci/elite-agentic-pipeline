# Interview Notes: Jen — Banh Mi Fusion Owner

**Date:** 2026-06-24  
**Interviewer:** UX Researcher (synthetic baseline for discovery)  
**Business:** Banh Mi Fusion — food cart + one brick-and-mortar location  
**Role:** Co-owner  
**Employees:** 8 across two locations  
**Annual revenue estimate:** ~$450K  
**Current online presence:** Instagram, DoorDash, UberEats, Squarespace site (rarely updated)  

## Jobs
- Maintain consistent branding and menu across two locations.
- Drive direct orders to reduce delivery-app commission.
- Manage catering requests for both locations without mixing them up.

## Pains
- Squarespace site is "fine" but takes too long to update; menu prices are stale.
- Two locations have different hours and sometimes different specials — confusing customers.
- Catering leads come to her personal phone and get lost in texts.

## Gains
- Wants a unified dashboard where she can update both locations.
- Likes bold, modern design; wants the site to "look like us, not a template."
- Interested in AI updates but wants approval before anything goes live.

## Direct Quotes
- "I don't need fancy. I need correct."
- "If AI can draft the update and I just hit approve, that's the sweet spot."
