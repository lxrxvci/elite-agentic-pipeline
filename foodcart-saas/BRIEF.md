# Product Brief

Foodcart SaaS: A no-code website builder for food carts and small restaurants. Businesses complete a quick onboarding flow, then connect or manually provide links to their digital presence: Google Business Profile, Yelp, DoorDash, UberEats, Grubhub, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, website (if any), menu URL, and logo/hero images. The platform ingests this data and generates a polished, mobile-first single-page restaurant website from a choice of premade templates inspired by three reference designs located at /Users/lxrxcvi/documents/kimi/workspaces/website-app/websites-templates: Banh Mi Fusion (bold diagonal branding, menu carousel, locations, catering), Real Indian Food (warm heritage storytelling, menu, catering), and Mis Abuelos (family/Mexican warmth). Each generated site has sections: hero, about/story, menu, locations/hours with live open/closed status, catering, contact, social links, and order links. Core SaaS features: tenant isolation (one subdomain per business, e.g. slug.foodcartsite.com), admin dashboard to edit business info, preview/publish, custom domain support (future), and subscription billing (future). AI Website Assistant: business owners can type natural-language requests like 'Add vegan section to menu' or 'Change hero headline to Summer Specials' or 'Update Friday hours to 11pm'; the assistant proposes a structured change preview, applies it only after explicit user approval, and is strictly harnessed: it cannot delete accounts, change billing, modify auth settings, execute arbitrary code, or access other tenants. All changes are versioned and revertible. Target stack: Next.js frontend, FastAPI backend, PostgreSQL, Clerk authentication, OpenAI/Anthropic LLM with structured output and guardrails.
